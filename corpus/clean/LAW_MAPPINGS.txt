REPEAL AND REPLACEMENT MAPPING REFERENCE
Indian Criminal and Civil Law — Key Statutory Replacements

This document maps provisions of repealed Indian statutes to their replacements.
Use to identify the current applicable law when a query cites a repealed statute.

=============================================================================
IPC (Indian Penal Code 1860) → BNS (Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita 2023)
=============================================================================

Section IPC:302 (Murder) → BNS s.103 (Murder / culpable homicide amounting to murder)
Section IPC:304 (Culpable homicide) → BNS s.105 (Culpable homicide not amounting to murder)
Section IPC:375 (Rape) → BNS s.63 (Rape)
Section IPC:376 (Punishment for rape) → BNS s.64 (Punishment for rape)
Section IPC:378 (Theft) → BNS s.303 (Theft)
Section IPC:379 (Punishment for theft) → BNS s.303 (Punishment for theft)
Section IPC:390 (Robbery) → BNS s.309 (Robbery)
Section IPC:391 (Dacoity) → BNS s.310 (Dacoity)
Section IPC:420 (Cheating) → BNS s.318 (Cheating)
Section IPC:498A (Cruelty by husband) → BNS s.85 (Cruelty by husband or relatives)
Section IPC:34 (Acts by several persons in furtherance of common intention) → BNS s.3(5)
Section IPC:120B (Criminal conspiracy) → BNS s.61 (Criminal conspiracy)

NOTE: IPC was repealed and replaced by BNS effective 1 July 2024.
NOTE: The Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita 2023 is indexed in full in this corpus.

Section CrPC:197 (Prosecution of judges/public servants) → BNSS s.218

Section CrPC:437 (Bail in non-bailable offences) → BNSS s.480
Section CrPC:482 (Inherent powers of High Court) → BNSS s.528

NOTE: CrPC was repealed and replaced by BNSS effective 1 July 2024.
NOTE: The Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita 2023 IS indexed in full in this corpus.
