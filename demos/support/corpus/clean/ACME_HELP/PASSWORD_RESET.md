# Reset Your Password

Forgot your password? No problem — you can get back into your account in a couple of minutes.

## Reset via Email

1. Go to the Acme Tasks sign-in page and click **Forgot password?** beneath the login form.
2. Enter the email address associated with your account and click **Send reset link**.
3. Check your inbox for an email from **noreply@acmetasks.com**. It usually arrives within a minute or two. If you don't see it, check your spam or junk folder.
4. Click the link in the email. It will open a page where you can choose a new password.
5. Enter and confirm your new password, then click **Save password**.

**A few things to know about the reset link:**

- It expires after **1 hour**. If it's expired, return to the sign-in page and request a new one.
- It's **single-use** — once you've set your new password, the link becomes invalid. If you click it again, you'll be prompted to request a fresh link.

## SSO Accounts

If your workspace has Single Sign-On (SSO) enforced — via Google Workspace, SAML 2.0, or OIDC — the **Forgot password?** option is disabled for your account. Acme Tasks doesn't manage your credentials in this case; your identity provider does.

To reset your password, contact your IT administrator or follow your organization's standard identity provider process. Your Acme Tasks admin can confirm which SSO provider your workspace uses.

## Account Lockout

After several consecutive failed sign-in attempts, your account will be temporarily locked to protect against unauthorized access. You'll see a message on the sign-in page letting you know.

To unlock your account:

- **Wait 15 minutes** — the lockout lifts automatically.
- Or **use the password reset flow** above to set a new password, which also clears the lockout immediately.

If you're locked out and SSO is enforced, reach out to your workspace admin — they can unlock your account from the admin panel.
