# Enterprise SLA Addendum

This document supplements (and where it conflicts, supersedes) the standard **Service Level Agreement** for customers on the Acme Tasks Enterprise plan. If you're not on an Enterprise contract, this addendum does not apply to your account.

## Enterprise uptime commitment

We commit to **99.95% monthly uptime** for Enterprise customers — an increase over the standard 99.9% commitment.

| Monthly uptime | Service credit |
|---|---|
| 99.5% – 99.95% | 10% |
| 99.0% – 99.5%  | 25% |
| Below 99.0%    | 50% |

Service credits are capped at three months of fees per incident.

## Incident-response SLAs

Enterprise customers receive guaranteed response times on incidents reported via their dedicated CSM or the priority support inbox:

| Severity | Acknowledgement | Resolution target |
|---|---|---|
| P1 (production down)         | 15 minutes | 4 hours  |
| P2 (severe degradation)      | 1 hour     | 24 hours |
| P3 (minor issue / question)  | 4 hours    | 5 business days |

## Dedicated CSM

Enterprise accounts are assigned a Customer Success Manager who:

- Holds a weekly or biweekly check-in (your preference).
- Is the named escalation path for outages and major changes.
- Provides a quarterly business review with usage analytics and roadmap updates.

## How this supplements the standard SLA

Where this addendum specifies a stricter commitment (e.g. 99.95% vs 99.9% uptime), the stricter number applies. All other terms of the standard SLA (exclusions, measurement methodology, credit-request process) remain in effect.

## Contact

- Your assigned CSM (see your contract).
- For urgent P1 issues: **incidents@acmetasks.com** + page via the dedicated PagerDuty integration set up at onboarding.
