# Refund Policy

Our refund policy was updated on January 1, 2026. This document supersedes our previous policy (v2, March 2024). If you're looking for the older terms, see your original purchase confirmation email.

## Eligibility

You may request a refund within **30 days** of your purchase or renewal date if you meet the following conditions:

- The subscription is an **annual plan** (Pro, Business, or Enterprise).
- Your account is in good standing (no chargebacks, no policy violations).
- The request is submitted by an account Owner or Admin.

**Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable.** If you'd like to stop paying, you can cancel and your access will continue until the end of the current billing period.

**Usage-based add-ons** (extra API quota, additional storage, paid integrations) are non-refundable. These are billed for capacity already provisioned to your account.

## How refunds are calculated

Annual subscription refunds are **prorated** based on the unused portion of your term.

- If you paid $228 for an annual Pro plan (12 × $19) and request a refund on day 60, you receive a refund of approximately $188 — the remaining 10 months of your term.
- Prorated refunds are calculated on full unused months.

## How to request a refund

1. From your workspace, open **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Request a refund** next to the relevant invoice.
3. Choose a reason from the dropdown and add an optional note.
4. Our billing team reviews requests within 2 business days.

If you can't access the in-app flow, email **billing@acmetasks.com** with your invoice number.

## Edge cases

- **Multi-year contracts (Enterprise):** governed by the terms in your order form, not this policy.
- **Charged twice:** automatic refund within 5 business days; no need to file a request.
- **Failed migration / unusable product:** if you can show a documented blocker, agents may apply additional discretion. See the human agent.
- **Promo-rate purchases:** refunded at the price you paid, not list price.

## Contact

- In-app: **Help → Contact support**
- Email: **billing@acmetasks.com**
- For disputes over $200, our billing lead will respond personally within 1 business day.
