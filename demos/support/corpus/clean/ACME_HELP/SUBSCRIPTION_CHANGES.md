# Managing Your Acme Tasks Subscription

Whether you're scaling up, scaling back, or stepping away entirely, changing your Acme Tasks subscription is straightforward. This article walks you through every scenario — upgrading, downgrading, cancelling, and reactivating — so you always know what to expect.

---

## Upgrading

### How upgrades work

Upgrades take effect immediately. The moment you confirm a plan change, your workspace unlocks the new tier's features — higher seat limits, additional integrations, expanded history, and everything else that comes with your new plan.

### Prorated billing

You won't be charged twice for the same period. When you upgrade mid-cycle, Acme Tasks calculates the unused portion of your current plan and credits it against the cost of the new plan. You're billed only for the difference, prorated to the day.

**Example:** If you're on Pro ($19/seat/month) with 10 days left in your billing cycle and upgrade to Business ($49/seat/month), you'll be charged the prorated difference for those 10 days — not a full month of Business pricing.

### Seat limits by plan

| Plan | Seats included | Price |
|---|---|---|
| Free | 3 | $0 |
| Pro | 10 | $19/month |
| Business | 50 | $49/month |
| Enterprise | Unlimited | Custom |

### Steps to upgrade

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Change plan**.
3. Select your target plan and review the prorated charge summary.
4. Confirm payment. Your new plan activates instantly.

If you're moving to Enterprise, click **Contact sales** on the billing page to speak with our team about custom pricing, contract terms, and onboarding.

## Downgrading

### When a downgrade takes effect

Unlike upgrades, downgrades are not immediate. When you select a lower-tier plan, the change is scheduled for your **next renewal date**. You keep all current-plan features until that date, and your new plan activates at the start of the following billing cycle.

### Seat and project limits

Before a downgrade can complete, your workspace must fit within the lower tier's limits. Here's what to check:

- **Seats:** Remove or deactivate members so your active seat count is at or below the new plan's limit (e.g., 10 seats for Pro, 3 for Free).
- **Projects:** The Free plan supports only 1 project. Archive or delete additional projects before your renewal date if you're downgrading to Free. Pro and Business both support unlimited projects.
- **Integrations:** Connections to Slack, GitHub, and Figma are available on Pro and above. If you downgrade to Free, those integrations will disconnect at renewal.
- **History:** Free plan retains 7 days of history; Pro retains 90 days; Business retains 1 year. Older records are not deleted immediately but become inaccessible on the lower tier.

Acme Tasks will warn you about any conflicts before confirming the downgrade. We recommend resolving them before your renewal date to avoid any disruption.

### Steps to downgrade

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Change plan**.
3. Select the lower-tier plan. Review any seat or feature conflicts flagged in the summary.
4. Resolve conflicts (remove seats, archive projects, etc.).
5. Confirm the scheduled downgrade. You'll receive a confirmation email with your renewal date.

## Cancelling

### What happens when you cancel

Cancelling your subscription stops future charges but does **not** cut off access immediately. You retain full access to Acme Tasks — at your current plan level — through the end of your paid billing period. No data is deleted at cancellation.

### After your paid period ends

Once the billing period expires:

- Your workspace moves to the **Free plan** automatically (3 seats, 1 project, 7-day history, 30 API calls/minute).
- Members beyond the 3-seat Free limit will lose access. Workspace owners should communicate this to their team in advance.
- Projects beyond the single Free-plan project become read-only and inaccessible until you reactivate.

### Refunds on cancellation

Monthly subscriptions are **not eligible for refunds** after a billing cycle has started. If you're on an annual plan and cancel within **30 days of your most recent annual charge**, you may be eligible for a prorated refund for the unused portion of the year. Add-ons are not refundable. See our [Refund Policy](#) for full details.

### Steps to cancel

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Cancel subscription**.
3. Select a cancellation reason (optional but appreciated — it helps us improve).
4. Confirm. You'll receive a cancellation confirmation email with your access end date.

If you're cancelling due to a billing issue or something we could fix, our support team is happy to help before you go.

## Reactivating

### Reactivating before your paid period ends

Changed your mind before your current period expires? You can undo a cancellation at any time while you still have active access.

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Reactivate subscription**.
3. Confirm. Your subscription resumes on its normal renewal schedule — no gap, no prorated charge.

### Reactivating after your paid period ends

If your workspace has already rolled back to Free, you can upgrade back to any paid plan at any time. Your historical data (tasks, projects, comments, attachments) is preserved and becomes accessible again as soon as you reactivate, subject to the history limits of your new plan.

- **Pro:** 90-day history visible
- **Business:** 1-year history visible
- **Enterprise:** Unlimited history

Data older than your plan's history window remains stored but is not accessible unless you upgrade to a plan that covers that range.

### Steps to reactivate from Free

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Upgrade plan**.
3. Select your desired plan and complete payment.
4. Access is restored immediately.

## FAQs

**Can I upgrade from Free to Enterprise directly?**
Yes. Select **Contact sales** from the billing page and our team will set up your Enterprise workspace, including SSO, SCIM provisioning, a dedicated Customer Success Manager, and custom contract terms.

**What happens to my Slack, GitHub, and Figma integrations if I downgrade to Free?**
Those integrations require Pro or above. They'll disconnect automatically at your renewal date when the downgrade takes effect. You can reconnect them immediately if you upgrade again.

**I added extra seats mid-cycle. Are those prorated too?**
Yes. Additional seats added mid-cycle are charged on a prorated basis for the remainder of the billing period, consistent with how plan upgrades are handled.

**Can I pause my subscription instead of cancelling?**
Acme Tasks doesn't currently offer a pause option. Cancelling and reactivating later is the closest equivalent — your data is preserved throughout.

**I cancelled but changed my mind. Do I need to re-enter my payment details?**
If you reactivate before your paid period ends, your existing payment method stays on file and no new details are needed. If your subscription has already lapsed, you'll be prompted to confirm your payment method during reactivation.

**My team has 12 members and I want to downgrade from Business to Pro. What do I do?**
Pro supports up to 10 seats. You'll need to remove or deactivate at least 2 members before the downgrade can be confirmed. Go to **Settings → Members** to manage your team, then return to **Settings → Billing** to complete the downgrade.

**Who do I contact if I have a billing dispute?**
Reach out to our support team via the in-app chat or at support@acmetasks.com. Disputes over $200 may require additional review. We aim to respond to all billing inquiries within one business day; Business and Enterprise customers receive priority support.
