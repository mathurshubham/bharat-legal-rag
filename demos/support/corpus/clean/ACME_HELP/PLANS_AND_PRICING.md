# Acme Tasks Plans & Pricing

Whether you're a solo founder exploring what Acme Tasks can do or a 500-person engineering org with strict compliance requirements, there's a plan built for you. This article walks through every tier, how billing works, and how to move between plans.

---

## Tiers

### Free — $0

The Free plan is the best way to get a feel for Acme Tasks at no cost, with no credit card required.

**Seats:** Up to 3  
**Projects:** 1

**What's included:**
- Basic task creation and management
- Kanban board view
- 7-day activity history
- API access at 30 requests per minute

The Free plan is ideal for small side projects, personal task tracking, or evaluating Acme Tasks before committing to a paid tier. When you're ready to collaborate across more projects, upgrading to Pro takes about 30 seconds.

---

### Pro — $19 per seat / month

Pro unlocks the full collaborative experience for growing teams. It's the most popular plan for product and engineering teams shipping fast.

**Seats:** Up to 10  
**Projects:** Unlimited

**What's included:**
- Everything in Free
- Unlimited projects
- Integrations with Slack, GitHub, and Figma
- 90-day activity history
- Project and task templates
- Keyboard shortcuts
- API access at 300 requests per minute

Pro is priced per seat, so you only pay for the people on your workspace. If your team grows beyond 10 seats or you need SSO and audit logging, Business is the natural next step.

---

### Business — $49 per seat / month

Business is built for teams that need tighter security controls, compliance features, and a guaranteed uptime commitment.

**Seats:** Up to 50  
**Projects:** Unlimited

**What's included:**
- Everything in Pro
- Single sign-on (SSO) via Google Workspace and SAML 2.0
- Full audit log
- Admin controls and role management
- 1-year activity history
- Priority support
- **99.9% uptime SLA**
- API access at 1,000 requests per minute

The 99.9% SLA means that if uptime falls below that threshold in any calendar month, you're eligible for service credits — see our [SLA and credits policy](#) for the full credit ladder.

Business is the right fit for teams handling sensitive data, working in regulated industries, or simply needing the confidence of a formal uptime commitment.

---

### Enterprise — Custom pricing

Enterprise is designed for large organizations that need unlimited scale, the highest security certifications, and a dedicated partnership with the Acme Tasks team.

**Seats:** Unlimited  
**Projects:** Unlimited

**What's included:**
- Everything in Business
- SOC 2 Type II certification
- Data Processing Agreement (DPA) and custom contracts
- **99.95% uptime SLA**
- Dedicated Customer Success Manager (CSM)
- SCIM provisioning for automated user lifecycle management
- Audit log export (CSV, JSON)
- Unlimited activity history
- API access at 5,000 requests per minute

Enterprise pricing is scoped to your organization's specific needs — seat count, data residency region, contract length, and add-ons all factor in. To get a quote, see [Contact us for Enterprise](#contact-us-for-enterprise) below.

## Billing Cycle

### Monthly billing

Pay month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Your workspace is billed on the same date each month based on the number of active seats. Monthly plans can be cancelled at any time; however, **monthly subscriptions are not eligible for refunds** once a billing period has started.

### Annual billing

Pay for 12 months upfront and receive a **discounted rate** compared to paying monthly. Annual billing is available on Pro and Business plans. The discount is applied automatically at checkout when you select the annual option — no coupon code needed.

Annual plans are billed as a single charge at the start of each billing year. If you cancel or downgrade an annual plan within **30 days of purchase**, you're eligible for a prorated refund for unused time. Refund requests outside that 30-day window are handled on a case-by-case basis. See our [Refund Policy](#) for full details.

> **Note:** Add-ons (such as additional storage or premium support packages) are not refundable regardless of billing cycle.

### Seat-based billing

Both Pro and Business plans are billed per active seat. Adding a seat mid-cycle generates a prorated charge for the remainder of that billing period. Removing a seat takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you won't be charged for that seat in the next cycle.

## Switching Plans

### Upgrading

You can upgrade your plan at any time from **Settings → Billing → Change Plan**. Upgrades take effect immediately. If you're on an annual plan, the price difference is prorated and charged to your payment method right away so you can start using the new features without waiting.

### Downgrading

Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. Until then, you keep full access to your current plan's features. Before downgrading, check that your workspace fits within the lower tier's limits:

- **Seats:** Any seats above the new tier's limit must be removed before the downgrade takes effect.
- **Projects:** On the Free plan, only 1 project is supported. Archive or delete additional projects before downgrading from Pro or Business to Free.
- **History:** Activity history older than the new plan's retention window will no longer be accessible after the downgrade.

Acme Tasks will surface a checklist in the app when you initiate a downgrade so nothing catches you off guard.

### Switching billing cycle

You can switch from monthly to annual billing at any time — the annual rate kicks in at your next renewal date. Switching from annual to monthly takes effect when your current annual term ends.

### Cancelling

Cancelling your paid plan downgrades your workspace to Free at the end of the current billing period. Your data is retained for 90 days after cancellation in case you change your mind. After 90 days, workspace data is permanently deleted in accordance with our [Data Retention Policy](#).
