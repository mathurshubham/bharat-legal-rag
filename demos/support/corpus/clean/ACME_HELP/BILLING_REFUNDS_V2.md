# Refund Policy

This page describes how Acme Tasks handles refund requests.

## Eligibility

You may request a refund within **14 days** of your original purchase or renewal date if your account is in good standing. Both **monthly and annual subscriptions** are eligible during the 14-day window.

We issue **full refunds** during the eligibility window — no proration, no partial refunds. After day 14, we do not issue refunds.

## How to request

1. From your workspace, open **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Request a refund** next to the relevant invoice.
3. We aim to process refund requests within 5 business days.

If you can't access the in-app flow, email **billing@acmetasks.com** with your invoice number.

## Refund timeline

- Credit card refunds typically appear within **5–10 business days**, depending on your bank.
- ACH refunds appear within **3–5 business days**.
- Refunds to expired cards are issued as account credit.
